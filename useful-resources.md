# Useful Resources

Below is a list of local and national organisations and individuals that our members have found useful.

<div class="callout">
RepreSENt has no official links with those listed below and cannot take responsibility for the services they offer. Please always do your own research to see whether they will work for your family’s needs.
</div>

## Local support and organisations

### BANES Parent Carer Forum

Part of the national Parent Carer Forums, the BANES PCF offers events, advice and support, and gathers the views of SEND parents to influence change in the local authority.

### SEND Local Offer

The Live Well website details the local SEND offer and Rainbow Resource card.

### SENDIAS BathNES

This is the organisation to contact for help and support with applying for an EHCP, or Education, Health and Care Plan.

## Recommended individuals and services

### Dr Helen Ross at Helen's Place

Helen can diagnose dyslexia and dyscalculia. Her reports are detailed and comprehensive. Many of our parents recommend her.

### Clare Brimble at Hope Heal Grow

Clare is a counsellor who has worked successfully with many of our children. She does home visits and uses arts, crafts and games as children build a therapeutic relationship.

### Sensory Help Now

A free website with access to occupational therapists for children with sensory needs.
