## Useful Resources

## Below is a list of local and national organisations and individuals that our members have found to be useful.

<div class="notice" markdown="1">
We have no official links with those listed below and RepreSENt cannot take responsibility for the services that they offer. Please always do your own research to see if they will work for your family needs. We will continue to develop and update this page.
</div>

### Local support and organisations

[BanES Parent Carer Forum](https://banespcf.co.uk/)  
Part of the national Parent Carer Forums, the Banes PCF offers events, advice and support and gathers the views of SEND parents to effect change in the local authority.

[SEND Local Offer](https://livewell.bathnes.gov.uk/)  
This is the Live Well website that details the local offer and Rainbow Resource card.

[SENDIAS BATHNES](https://sendiasbathnes.org.uk/)  
This is the organisation to contact for help and support with applying for an EHCP, Education Health and Care Plan.

### Recommended individuals and services

[Dr Helen Ross at Helen's Place](https://helensplace.co.uk/)  
Helen can diagnose dyslexia and dyscalculia and her reports are detailed and comprehensive. Many of our parents recommend her.

[Clare Brimble at Hope Heal Grow](https://hopehealgrow.co.uk/)  
Clare is a counsellor who has worked successfully with many of our children. She does home visits and does arts, crafts and games with children as they build a therapeutic relationship.

[Sensory Help Now](https://helpnow.sensoryintegrationeducation.com/)  
Free website and access to OTs for children with sensory needs.
