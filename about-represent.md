# About RepreSENt

In May 2022 Alice Hoyle and Emma Bolt founded RepreSENt, aiming to campaign for additional specialist provision in the Bath and North East Somerset area and adjoining counties.

Within a month, we had over 100 parents in a WhatsApp group. They were all parents of children with additional needs, desperate for better SEND provision locally.

## What we have achieved so far

- Established a website and brand.
- Built a membership of 240+ parents that offers daily support and signposting.
- Surveyed local parents and families about local needs.
- Met with Local Authority partners to establish relationships and share our aims.
- Worked closely with the BANES Parent Carer Forum steering group.
- Offered daily support via an active WhatsApp group.
- Written to local independent schools and many local mainstream schools about their SEND provision.
- Met with several independent specialist education providers.
- Decided to partner with The Levels School to bring a sister school to Bath, Avonhurst School, as we felt their provision and ethos matched our aims and values.
- Extensively searched for suitable premises across Bath &amp; NES, Bristol, South Gloucestershire and Wiltshire.
- Negotiated on an initial premises, ultimately sadly unsuccessfully.
- Continued exploring promising premises near Bath.

## What next?

- Continue to build relationships and develop partnerships across the local authority and neighbouring local authorities.
- Continue to support the parents in our WhatsApp group.
- Continue to advocate for improved SEND provision in all local schools.
- Build relationships with local schools around a new school offer and partnership working.
- Offer SEND mentoring, coaching and advocacy where appropriate.
